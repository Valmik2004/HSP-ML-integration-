Our Project:
Smart Hydroponic Radish Microgreens

Revolutionary Hydroponic System

The HydroFarm project revolutionizes radish microgreens cultivation through an automated hydroponic ecosystem. Our innovative solution integrates advanced IoT sensors, machine learning algorithms, and precision automation.

Real-time monitoring of temperature, humidity, pH levels, nutrient concentration, and light intensity ensures optimal growing conditions. The intelligent pump system delivers nutrients based on predictive plant needs, maximizing yield while minimizing resource consumption.
